IZ*ONE

This girlband name is IZ*ONE. They are a K-Pop girl group based on South Korea, consist 12 members in the group. So far they have a total of 7 albums, 4 albums in Korean and 3 albums in Japanese. They made their first debut on October 29th 2018 with their extended play (EP) called Color*iz
